What is DevOps?

DevOps is a collaborative approach to software development that combines development (Dev) and operations (Ops) teams. It aims to shorten the development lifecycle, improve software quality, and deliver features faster.

Benefits of DevOps

  • Faster deployments
  • Improved collaboration
  • Higher quality software
  • Increased reliability
